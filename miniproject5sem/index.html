About Us



Kolhapur Jaggery Tea is a traditional, rich, and sweet tea prepared using jaggery instead of refined sugar. A perfect blend of spices and sweetness, this tea is a favorite among locals in Kolhapur, Maharashtra. It’s made with the finest jaggery sourced directly from Kolhapur, giving it a rich caramel-like flavor.
